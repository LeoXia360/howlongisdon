how long is don?

about:

Walking by the popular food truck, Don, we noticed that the line is constantly full and people become hesitant to wait to order the scrumptious food that the food truck offers. It was then that we decided that a solution was necessary. The fruit of our efforts, www.HowLongIsDon.info was born. Our intelligent app tells you how long you have to wait in order to get your food at Don.
